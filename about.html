BELLUGA VOGUE

Our Story

FOUNDED 2019

Born from Boldness

Belluga Vogue was founded in 2019 by Adaeze Okonkwo, a Nigerian-born fashion designer who believed African women deserved luxury fashion that celebrated their strength, beauty and bold individuality.

Born in Lagos and trained at Central Saint Martins in London, Adaeze built Belluga Vogue from a single conviction: that bold is not a statement — it's a way of life.

What began as a small Lagos atelier has grown into a global brand with collections stocked in boutiques across Nigeria, the United Kingdom and the United States. Today, Belluga Vogue dresses women who lead — in boardrooms, on red carpets, and in every room they choose to command.

CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Adaeze Okonkwo

Adaeze

Founder & Creative Director

Adaeze studied at Central Saint Martins in London before cutting her teeth in the luxury fashion houses of Milan and Paris. After a decade shaping collections for global brands, she returned to Lagos to build something entirely her own.

She splits her time between Lagos and London, drawing inspiration from both the vibrant textures of West Africa and the tailored precision of European couture. Her work has been featured in Vogue Africa, Elle Nigeria and Arise Magazine.

Our World

What started in a Lagos atelier is now worn across three continents. Belluga Vogue has a growing presence in Lagos, London and New York — with each city informing our aesthetic in its own way.

From the bold prints of West Africa to the structured tailoring of European fashion capitals, every Belluga Vogue piece carries the fingerprint of a global creative vision rooted in African excellence.
